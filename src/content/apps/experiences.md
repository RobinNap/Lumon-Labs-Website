## About Experiences

Experiences is more than a travel journal — it's a way to capture the essence of your adventures. From weekend getaways to once-in-a-lifetime trips, preserve your memories beautifully.

## Key Features

- **Beautiful Journal Entries** — Add photos, notes, and locations to each experience
- **Interactive Maps** — See all your travels visualized on a beautiful map
- **Photo Integration** — Seamlessly imports photos from your Camera Roll
- **Location Tagging** — Automatically or manually tag where memories happened
- **iCloud Sync** — Your memories, available on all your devices
- **Privacy Focused** — All data stored locally or in your iCloud account

## Capture Every Moment

- Restaurant discoveries
- Hidden gems and local favorites
- Breathtaking viewpoints
