## About Motif Designer

Motif Designer is your creative companion for crafting beautiful patterns and motifs. Whether you're designing for textiles, wallpapers, or digital art, our intuitive tools help you bring your vision to life.

## Key Features

- **Intuitive Pattern Creation** — Design complex patterns with simple, powerful tools
- **Symmetry Tools** — Create perfectly balanced designs with radial, reflective, and rotational symmetry
- **Export Options** — Export in multiple formats for print and digital use
- **iCloud Sync** — Access your designs across all your Apple devices
- **Dark Mode** — Beautiful interface that adapts to your system preferences

## Perfect For

- Textile and fabric designers
- Graphic artists and illustrators
- Surface pattern designers
- Anyone who loves creating beautiful designs

- iOS 17.0 or later
- iPadOS 17.0 or later
- macOS 14.0 or later
