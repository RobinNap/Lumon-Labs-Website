## About Aviary

Experience aviation in real time. Aviary combines live air traffic control audio with real-time aircraft tracking on interactive maps, bringing the aviation world to your fingertips.

## Key Features

- **Live Air Traffic Control Audio** — Listen to live ATC feeds from airports worldwide
- **Real-Time Aircraft Tracking** — Track aircraft positions on interactive maps in real time
- **Airport Lookup** — Search and find airports by IATA codes or names
- **Intelligent Search** — Prioritizes exact matches (e.g., IATA codes like "SFO", "AMS") and shows the most relevant results first
- **Interactive Maps** — Explore aircraft positions with detailed, interactive maps
- **Multiple Channel Selection** — Choose from various tower, ground, and approach frequencies

## Perfect For

- Aviation enthusiasts
- Pilots and flight students
- Air traffic control observers
- Anyone interested in real-time aviation data
